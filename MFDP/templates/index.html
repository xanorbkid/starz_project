Who We Are

The Ministry of Finance and Development Planning (MFDP) in Liberia is a government institution responsible for overseeing fiscal policy, budget preparation, economic planning, and various financial and development-related matters in the country. It is headed by the Minister of Finance and Development PlanningHon. Samuel Tweh, who is a senior government official appointed by the President of Liberia.
